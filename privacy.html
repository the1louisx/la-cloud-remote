Privacy Policy

LA Cloud Remote

1. Data Controller

LA Software UG
Berlin, Germany

2. Data We Process

LA Cloud Remote processes the minimum data needed to operate the service:

  • Device & security identifiers — a random device ID, authentication tokens (device token, session token, pairing token), and a hashed PIN. Your PIN is never stored or transmitted in plaintext.
  • Usage metrics — per-device timestamps such as first seen, last seen, and arm/disarm events, used to measure retention and reliability.
  • Technical logs — IP address and user-agent string, collected automatically by our hosting infrastructure for rate limiting and abuse prevention.

We do not collect email addresses, names, or any account information. There are no user accounts.

3. Purpose of Processing

  • Service operation — enabling remote arm and disarm commands between your phone and laptop.
  • Abuse prevention — rate limiting and escalating lockouts to protect against brute-force attacks.
  • Reliability & retention — measuring how the service is used so we can improve it.

4. Legal Basis

We process data under the following legal bases (GDPR):

  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) — operating the service, preventing abuse, and improving reliability.
  • Contract necessity (Art. 6(1)(b)) — processing required to provide the remote control functionality you use.

5. Data Retention

  • Pairing tokens — approximately 2 minutes (automatically deleted after use or expiry).
  • Session tokens — up to 24 hours.
  • Device registrations — removed after 24 hours of inactivity.
  • Usage metrics — retained for up to 180 days.
  • Server logs — retained for up to 30 days by our hosting provider.

6. Data Sharing

  • Our backend is hosted on Render.com, which acts as a data processor.
  • The phone interface is served via GitHub Pages.
  • We do not sell your data to anyone.
  • There are no third-party tracking scripts, advertising, or analytics cookies.

7. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of data associated with your device ID.
  • Deletion — request removal of your data at any time.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Complaint — lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (e.g., the Berlin Commissioner for Data Protection).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through our website.
